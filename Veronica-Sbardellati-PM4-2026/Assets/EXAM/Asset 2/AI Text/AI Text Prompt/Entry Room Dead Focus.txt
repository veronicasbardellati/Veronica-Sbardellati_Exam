This is a Unity controller for a simple interaction. It uses the Trigger Sensor existing in the project. When the player is detected it plays the interaction.
The interaction will have several layered feedback each its own Dotween animation. For each Dotween animation expose the curve in the inspector.

Fields:
- Audio Source
	- pitch entry duration
	- pitch entry curve

- Vignette intensity and desaturation (from post processing)


Methods:
-On play
	- the pitch of the audio source goes from 0 to 1 over some duration (exposed) and with the aforementioned curve.

Scene lighting gradually turn on following the same behavior.
It's comprised on the following variables:
	- intensity Multiplier of the Environment Lighting (goes from 0 to 1)
	- intensity Multiplier of the Reflection (goes from 0 to 1)
